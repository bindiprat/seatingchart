What's on the Menu

Cocktail Appetizers

  • Aloo Tiki Chole: A crispy potato patty (aloo tikki) served with spiced chickpeas (chole), topped with tangy tamarind chutney and yogurt.
  • Papadi Chaat: Crispy wafers topped with potatoes, chickpeas, yogurt, and tamarind chutney for a sweet-spicy-tangy burst of flavor.
  • Dabeli: A Gujarati street food sandwich with spicy mashed potatoes, pomegranate, peanuts, and chutneys in a toasted bun.
  • Veggie Quesadillas: Flour tortillas filled with mixed vegetables and cheese, grilled until golden and melty.
  • Edamame Pot Sticker: Pan-fried dumplings filled with a savory blend of edamame and vegetables, served with soy dipping sauce.
  • Toasted Spinach and Feta Phyllo Wraps: Flaky phyllo pastry wrapped around a warm spinach and tangy feta cheese filling.

Dinner

  • Kandhvi: Thin rolls made from gram flour and yogurt, seasoned with mustard seeds and coconut — a light and tangy Gujarati delicacy.
  • Surti Pattice: A stuffed potato cutlet filled with spiced coconut and dry fruits, crispy on the outside and soft inside.
  • Vegetable Pulao: A fragrant basmati rice dish cooked with mixed vegetables, whole spices, and herbs.
  • Kadhi: A yogurt-based curry thickened with gram flour and tempered with curry leaves, mustard seeds, and chilies.
  • Stuffed Bhinda: Okra stuffed with a spiced gram flour mix and sautéed until tender and flavorful.
  • Kadhai Paneer: Paneer cubes cooked with bell peppers, onions, and tomatoes in a spiced kadhai masala.
  • Surti Undhiyu: A slow-cooked Gujarati mixed vegetable dish with fenugreek dumplings, yams, and eggplant in aromatic spices.
  • Puri/Naan Mix: A selection of puffed deep-fried puris and soft, oven-baked naan breads.
  • Papaad, Pickle, Salad: Crunchy lentil crisps, tangy Indian pickles, and a refreshing salad assortment.

Desserts

  • Assorted Halwa: A variety of Indian halwas such as suji, moong dal, or carrot — warm, rich, and ghee-laced.
  • Rasmalai: Soft paneer discs soaked in saffron- and cardamom-flavored sweetened milk.
  • Cake: Classic layered cake, fluffy and frosted, offered in celebratory flavors.
  • Chocolate Chip Cookies: Chewy, buttery cookies loaded with semi-sweet chocolate chips.
  • Churros: Crispy fried dough pastries dusted with cinnamon sugar, served with chocolate sauce.
